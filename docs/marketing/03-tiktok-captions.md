## 🎬 Video editing guidelines

1. **First 2 seconds = hook.** Cut straight to the funniest moment. Don't introduce yourself.
2. **Subtitles always.** 80% of TikTok is watched muted. Use CapCut auto-subtitles.
3. **End with CTA card.** Text overlay last 2 sec: *"Haz la tuya en vacilada.com"*
4. **Don't show phone numbers.** Blur or bleep.
5. **If victim is identifiable, get consent on camera before posting** (or blur face).
6. **Never post a prank that goes too dark.** Suspensions happen fast in the TikTok comedy niche.
