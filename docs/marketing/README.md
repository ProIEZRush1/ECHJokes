## Order of operations

### Day -7 to -1 (pre-launch)
1. Claim all handles (bios in `01-social-bios.md`)
2. Create 5 prank calls yourself, save the best moments
3. Edit into 30-sec vertical videos
4. Pre-schedule 7 days of content

### Day 0 (launch)
Follow `00-launch-day-checklist.md` step by step.

### Day 1–30
- Post 1 video/day per platform (`02-content-calendar.md` + `03-tiktok-captions.md`)
- 5 influencer DMs/day (`05-influencer-outreach.md`)
- 1 Reddit/FB group post/week per community (`06-community-hijacking.md`)
- 1 press pitch/week (`07-press-release.md`)

## Key metrics to track weekly

| Metric | Target week 4 |
|---|---|
| TikTok followers | 5K |
| Instagram followers | 2K |
| Users registered | 500 |
| Users who made ≥1 call | 150 |
| Referrals completed | 30 |
| Revenue (MXN) | $5K |

If any of these are <50% of target at week 2, stop and reassess — something isn't clicking and more volume won't fix it.

## Content production pipeline (repeatable system)

1. **Record** prank calls yourself with Vacilada (daily, 2-3 calls)
2. **Filter** the recordings — pick the top 20-sec moment of each
3. **Edit** in CapCut: subtitles + end card
4. **Schedule** via Buffer/Later for all 5 platforms (TikTok, IG Reels, YT Shorts, FB Reels, X)
5. **Engage** with first 10 comments in first hour

Takes ~30 min/day once you have the rhythm.

## Paid ads (WHEN to start)

**Don't run ads until:**
- You have 10+ organic videos with >10K views each (proves content works)
- Your share-to-signup funnel converts ≥5% (if people share but don't sign up, ads won't save you)
- You have ≥500 MXN LTV per user (so ads can pay back at ~$2 CAC)

Then: start with **Meta ads** targeting Mexico 18-35 with "humor" interest. Boost your best-performing organic videos. Budget: $100 MXN/day for 14 days = $1,400 MXN test.

Skip Google Ads for a consumer entertainment product — intent is wrong.
